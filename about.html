The Context Window

The Context Window is a monthly dispatch from inside a particular kind of conversation — the one happening right now between you and the machine that just finished your sentence. It is not a tutorial. It will not teach you to write better prompts. It is interested in the subtler, stranger, and solitary residue of working alongside AI — something that knows everything and remembers nothing, apologizes constantly and means it never, and answers the same question for the seventeenth time with the enthusiasm of the first. Some of it is funny. All of it is true.

About Anindya

Anindya spends a large part of his working life inside AI conversations — long sessions of planning, debating, and extrapolating. The Context Window is where the leftover thinking goes: the moments too strange or too funny to fit into a status update. It is about what it actually feels like to think next to a machine instead of just using one.
